DiscoverEnglish

Emory's English department features vibrant academic programs in literary and cultural studies, creative writing, and rhetoric and composition. We are committed to training students to be excellent writers, readers, researchers, and interpreters of our changing world.

The English department is committed to promoting racial justice and the goals of the Black Lives Matter movement through our work as teachers, scholars, and lifelong learners. Learn more about our commitment.
